Modular and Manufactured Housing in Florida

In Florida, modular housing and manufactured housing follow different codes. Our modular homes comply with the Florida Building Code and are set up on-site after being manufactured in a controlled factory environment. On the other hand, our manufactured homes follow the HUD Code and are transported as complete units to the site.

Benefits of Modular Housing in Tightly Packed Areas:

  • Efficient construction with factory precision.
  • Consistent quality control for durability and safety.
  • Customizable designs to fit limited spaces.
  • Eco-friendly practices for sustainability.

Benefits of Manufactured Housing in Tightly Packed Areas:

  • Quick setup for urgent housing needs.
  • Affordability compared to traditional options.
  • Some options offer mobility for future use or relocation.

The choice between them depends on factors like local regulations, design preferences, budget, and permanence desired. Always ensure compliance with relevant rules and seek professional guidance for the best fit in each situation.
