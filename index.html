Attanable Homes Solutions

Attainable Homes Inc. is a leader in the manufacturing of patented, steel Structural Insulated Panels (SIPs) and other related products. Our company manufactures the Structural Insulated Panel, a structurally robust, yet environmentally friendly, sustainable, fully recyclable building product and system. Our innovative panel system is used as a cost effective, energy efficient and disaster tolerant alternative to conventional building materials.
