Manufactured Homes

Taking advantage of world class technology and innovative solutions as well as design, our HUD constructed manufactured houses provide some of the most cost and energy efficient homes on today's market. Best of all, they are completely non-combustible. This U.S Patent-Protected building system exceeds 130MPH wind loads per NFPA-501.

Modular Homes

Our Florida Building Code constructed modular homes are a modern and innovative approach to residential construction, offering a versatile and efficient housing solution. These homes are built in sections or modules within a controlled factory setting, where they undergo rigorous quality control measures. The modules are then transported to the building site, where they are set-up to create a fully functional and aesthetically appealing home. The multi-stemmed prestressed transportable concrete floor doubles as the foundation, thereby eliminating the need for a site built foundation. This U.S Patent-Protected building system exceeds 175MPH wind loads per Florida Building Code.

Applications:

• Single Family Housing
• Multi Family Housing
• Accessory Dwelling Units
• Sub Divisions
• Manufactured Home Parks
• Infill Lots
