Fully Integrated Structural Building System (FISBS)

A structural wall / roof system and a structural aluminum framing system and a multi- stemmed pre-stressed concrete floor that can be transported without the use of a trailer or crane. The system withstands hurricane force winds in exceeding 150 mph, is non- combustible, is highly energy efficient, is completely GREEN construction and has the appearance of conventional site-built buildings.

This is a complete building system and does not require a site-built foundation or extraneous tie down devices.

System provides:

  • Concealed locking beam
  • Complete structural diaphragm through the steel skins
  • Insulation with an R24 and R28 thermal rating
  • Integral Electrical Chase
  • Complete structural frame
  • Complete structural foundation

Component Parts Include:

  • Metal Skins
  • Urethane Foam Insulation
  • Concealed locking Beam
  • Structural Aluminum Frame
  • Multi-stemmed

Structural Wall / Roof System

A structural wall / roof system comprised of 24” wide x 3” or 3.5” metal faced insulated sections with an integral rectangular electrical chase. The sections are joined together on the long parallel edges with a concealed locking beam that provides the structural capacity to withstand high wind loads. The Wall / Roof system requires a structural frame for support and is designed and engineered as a component of the Attainable Homes Fully Integrated Structural Building System (FISBS).

Must be connected to a structural foundation that is anchored to the ground or that is anchored with extraneous tie down devices.

System provides:

  • Concealed locking beam
  • Complete structural diaphragm through the steel skins
  • Insulation with an R24 and R28 thermal rating
  • Integral Electrical Chase

Component Parts Include:

  • Metal Skins
  • Urethane Foam Insulation
  • Concealed locking Beam

Rigid Frame Structural Wall / Roof System

A structural wall / roof system and a structural rigid frame comprised of metal faced insulated sections, a concealed locking beam, a structural frame and a rectangular electrical chase. The system is self-supporting and does not require an additional support frame. The wall / roof system is designed for use as the main wind force resisting system (MWFRS) when included in the Attainable Homes FISBS.

Must be connected to a structural foundation that is anchored to the ground or that is anchored with extraneous tie down devices.

System provides:

  • Concealed locking beam
  • Complete structural diaphragm through the steel skins
  • Insulation with an R24 and R28 thermal rating
  • Integral Electrical Chase
  • Complete structural frame

Component Parts Include:

  • Metal Skins
  • Urethane Foam Insulation
  • Concealed locking Beam
  • Structural Aluminum Frame
